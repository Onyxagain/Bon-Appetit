BON APPETIT

an introduction to my own media

Bon Appetit on this website of mine will be a sort of wikipedia of a game i hope to make in the future, this game is a 2d->3d rpg and horror game ,along with a visual novel provided by me.This Novel is based in a different world of species known as Desserts- beings based off sweet treats and food - like cakes and cookies-but unfortunately, the world has swarmed with a new species - The Rotten . Cannibals who desire to eat the dessert species,Luring them into traps and more.

In this novel, You play as a Dessert(you can have yourself a self insert if wanted,or one that would be ready for you) trying to find work,as you sign up to work at a restaurant - Butterscotches. the story unravels from there as you get to know people and watch the plot unfold.

WARNING!

this media includes horror aspects, you may see disrupting content involving topics of unrealistic gore(drawn),body horror and cannibalism,please be mindful.
